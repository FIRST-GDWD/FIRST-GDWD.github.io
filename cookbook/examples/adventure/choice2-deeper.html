The Quest to Slay the Dragon

Story

Who knew that going deeper into the dark forest would be a good idea? Because not long after, the trees give way to a vast golden plain, leading up to an imposing, craggy mountain.

You pass through the plains, the flourishing vegatation giving way to gravel and stone as you get closer to the mountain range.

As you reach the base of the mountain near a large cave, you find it is littered with the charred bones of fallen warriors who came before, their armor, shields, and weapons discarded on the ground.

Suddenly, you hear an ear-shattering roar echoing out from the cave. Then you see it: a majestic and fearsome dragon striding out from within. It sniffs the air, and then its eyes lock onto you.
