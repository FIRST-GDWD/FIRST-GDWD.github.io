The Quest to Slay the Dragon

Story

You decide that it was a poor decision to knock on the door, and flee from the hideous old woman and her hut.

However, this was an equally poor decision. Didn't anyone ever tell you not to reject a witch's invitation?

You hear her cackling loudly behind you. Suddenly, you trip on a vine on the ground. Wait, no, you didn't trip. IT tripped YOU.

Vines curl around your legs and up your torso, dragging you back towards the garden. A monstrous carnivorous plant, the source of the vines, unearths itself from the ground, and the last thing you see is its gaping maw open wide as you are dragged towards...
