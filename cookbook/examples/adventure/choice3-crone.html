The Quest to Slay the Dragon

Story

It turns out that despite her hideous appearance, your host is quite a nice lady. Guess you should learn to not judge by appearance.

Over a hot cup of tea with honey, she tells a tragic tale of how her son was also "chosen" to slay the dragon, and she never saw him again. She was getting up there in years and had a bad back, and with her son gone, she couldn't maintain the yard or the garden anymore.

After hearing her tale, and knowing that you couldn't return to your farm without evidence of slaying the dragon, you decide to stay here and take care of the old woman and her home.
