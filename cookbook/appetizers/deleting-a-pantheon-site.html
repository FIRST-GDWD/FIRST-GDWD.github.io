Deleting a Pantheon Site

Sometimes, you just want to start again from a clean slate

  1. First off, make sure you're VERY sure you want to delete your site. This process can't be reversed once you kick it off.

    You're really sure you want to delete your site? Okay, then let's continue.

    If you haven't already, log in to your Pantheon account, and navigate to the Sites tab in the left navbar.

    From here, click on the name of the Pantheon site that you want to delete.

  2. This will take you to the Pantheon Site Dashboard for that site.

    To begin the process of deleting your site, click the Settings tab in the top-right of the screen.

  3. This will switch you to the Settings view.

    From here, scroll all the way down the page to the section labeled "Delete site".

    Click the [Delete] button

  4. You might think that clicking [Delete Site] would delete your site...but no.

    They have an additional safeguard in place to prevent accidental deletion of your site: you need to manually type out the name of the site you want to delete.

    They want to be REALLY sure that you actually want to delete your site.

    Once you've typed out the name of your Pantheon site EXACTLY, the [Delete This Site] button will become enabled.

    Click that button to truly kick off the process of deleting your Pantheon site.

  5. After clicking the [Delete This Site] button, the popup will close, and you'll be returned to the Pantheon Dashboard.

    Your site is being deleted in the background behind the scenes.

    After a few minutes, check back on the Sites tab, and you should see that the site you wanted to delete is no longer in the Sites list.

Congrats! You've successfully deleted your Pantheon site!
