It's Critique Time!

Here are some ground rules for critiques in this class

FOR THOSE RECEIVING CRITIQUES

  • Assume good intent
  • Remember that your work is distinct from you
  • A criticism against your work is not an attack on you

FOR THOSE GIVING CRITIQUES

  • Be kind, and be helpful
  • Be as objective as possible, and focus on the Elements and Principles of Design
  • Provide at least one positive piece of feedback,
    and one suggestion for how to make it better
  • Be SPECIFIC in your feedback: specify the page, the location, the text, etc. that you're referring to
  • Be sure to include the WHY behind your comments: why do you think something looks good? why do you think your suggestion could make it better?
