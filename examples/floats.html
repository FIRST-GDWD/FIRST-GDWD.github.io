Let's take a look at how weird floats are

The goat image is semi-transparent here. Note how the text wraps around the image at narrow widths, but also note how the colored box of the paragraph goes BEHIND the image, even though the text does not.

It's kind of like the colored box of the paragraph is a liquid, and the float-ed image is a rock jutting up out of the liquid. It's there in the liquid, but doesn't move, while the stuff floating on the surface (the words of the paragraph) flow around it. Not the most perfect analogy, but probably as close as we can get.
